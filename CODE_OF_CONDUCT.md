# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful community. Please:
- Be kind and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Assume good intent; disagree with ideas, not people.
- Avoid harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks.
- Respect privacy; do not post private or sensitive information.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers. Consequences may include warnings, temporary bans, or removal from the community.

By participating, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.
